About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Leo Kerivin, I currently live in Montreal, Canada but I do not rule out traveling to other places to learn and discover new styles.

A few years ago I became interested in tattoos and I have always wanted to be able to dedicate myself to it, at the moment I have a small studio where I make designs and tattoo people interested in my designs.

The studio is not very large, but I have endeavored to create an area with a calm atmosphere and with my unique style to work comfortably with clients.

I enjoy creating and representing characters in absurd situations or with strange feelings.
